### Scientific Reality
- Neck skin is thinner than facial skin
- Fewer oil glands = drier, more vulnerable
- Constant movement (head turning) accelerates aging
- Frequently neglected in skincare routines
- More susceptible to sun damage

### The "Neglected Zone" Narrative

- Face creams aren't formulated for neck skin

- Anti-aging messaging focused on face
- Neck aging wasn't discussed until recently

## Emerging Ingredients & Research

### Peptides
- Signal skin to produce more collagen
- Specific peptides target firming/lifting

### Retinoids
- Gold standard for cell turnover
- Proven to reduce wrinkles
